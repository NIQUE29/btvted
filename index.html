BACHELOR OF TECHNICAL EDUCATION Major in Food Service Management

PROMOTION

The Bachelor of Technical-Vocational Teacher Education (BTVTEd) major in Food & Service Management (FSM) is a program designed to improve food and service managers' knowledge, skills, and work attitude in accordance with industry needs. It covers food selection and preparation, meal management, and fundamental and advanced food service management competencies. The course is designed to support students prepare for careers in the food service industry.
